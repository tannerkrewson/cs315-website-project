5 Dog Care Tips

  1. Protect your dog from common household hazards.
  2. Buy toys for your dog so they don't chew on your shoes.
  3. Allow you dog to socialize early in its life.
  4. Don't hit your dog; gently scold it verbly instead.
  5. If it is your first dog, choose a more submissive breed.
