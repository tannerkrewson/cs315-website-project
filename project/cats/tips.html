5 Cat Care Tips

  1. Cats require constant grooming.
  2. Avoid pairing small kittens with small kids.
  3. Be careful when introducing a new cat into a home with other animals.
  4. Make sure to give your cat a monthly dosage of parasite control.
  5. Buy a scratching post to save your couch from being destroyed.
